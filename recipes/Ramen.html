Ramen

Description

Ramen is a noodle dish, and happens to be one of the most well-known and beloved food staples in Japan!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Marinated beef or chicken
  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Kimchi
  • Cucumbers
  • Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Other vegetables you like!

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat for about 30 mins
  2. Prepare and cook the spinach
  3. Rinse and peel the Carrots
  4. Rinse and clean the shiitake mushrooms then thinly slice them
  5. Make sunny sideup fried eggs (personally recommend the runny kind!)
  6. Put the rice into the bowl and add meat, assorted vegetables then the egg on top of the rice!
