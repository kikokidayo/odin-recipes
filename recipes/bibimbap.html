Bibimbap

Description

Having been crowned as my favourite dish recently for its diverse nutrition and good, wholesome taste, Bibimbapis one of the most well-known and beloved dishes in Korea!

Bibimbap simply translates to mixed rice with meat and assorted vegetables , whic is reflected in its highly colorful, rich presentation!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Marinated beef or chicken
  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Kimchi
  • Cucumbers
  • Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Other vegetables you like!

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat for about 30 mins
  2. Prepare and cook the spinach
  3. Rinse and peel the Carrots
  4. Rinse and clean the shiitake mushrooms then thinly slice them
  5. Make sunny sideup fried eggs (personally recommend the runny kind!)
  6. Put the rice into the bowl and add meat, assorted vegetables then the egg on top of the rice!
